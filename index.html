About Lido Paddle Sports

Lido Paddle Sports was founded by Bobby who has lived in SoCal for the last two decades. His love of water sports and the ocean comes from growing up in Hawaii where he was a professional surfer. In his business today, he instills the values he learned while growing up in Hawaii: always be kind and respect nature.
